What is hololive?

Hololive Productions, more commonly known as just Hololive, is an idol company run by Cover Corp

Hololive's main headqirters is located in Japan. However, they have branches in Indonesia and China(former). The first idol was Tokino Sora, who debuted in September 7 2017, the company has seen some major growth in numbers and popularity. There are over 50 idols in Hololive, each with their own character design, voice, pesonality, outfits and even custom emojis(on YouTube).
