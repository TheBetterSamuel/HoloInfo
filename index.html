What is hololive?

Hololive Productions, more commonly known as just Hololive, is an idol company run by Cover Corp

Hololive's main headqirters is located in Japan. However, they have branches in Indonesia and China(former). The first idol was Tokino Sora, who debuted in September 7 2017, the company has seen some major growth in numbers and popularity. They have gained so much popularity that their most popular idol, Gawr Gura, has over 2 million subscribers on Youtube and 700 thousand followers on Twitter.

There are over 50 idols in Hololive, each with their own character design, voice, pesonality, outfits and even custom emojis(on YouTube). There are 5 sub-groups: Hololive(Main Group), Hololive English, Hololive Indonesia, Hololive China(ended) and Holostars. They also have a music label called INoNaKa Music.
